The Tallest Mountains in the World are the Himalayas

This is the place where I do my writing! Pretty cool, eh?

I think it's pretty cool! I write some unintelligible nonsense, and people eat it up! That's my favorite thing about writing, honestly. It's the fact that people will just assume you're intelligent if you can put pen to paper, so to speak.

Everyone should try it! Put on your pseudo-intellectual cap and see where it takes you! You may surprise yourself with how convincing you can be.

The only danger you might run into is offending people with your incredible pretensiousness. So make sure that you're smarter than the people that you write for, so that they never catch on to your act.

Thank you for reading! After all, I do it all for attention, so you've done me a great favor.
